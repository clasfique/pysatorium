Apparently, i have decided to write something that i call ataxia of the head\
quite an interesting thing that i have created to describe situations when you actually begin to lose control over your flow of motion in your mind, which, certainly, isn't the nicest thing to occur in one's life or existing.\
basically, i have personally experienced it many times , especially when i was getting exhausted by everything that is going around me\
it's a very abstractive term thought, because it isn't seen in person, but rather with how one behaves

and it's hard to tell if a one has gotten the "disease" which i created in my mind, but i assume that it happens when a person begins to do things that are not usual for them, but they do it because they want to find an exist\
for example it could be trying out new things or trying something you are afraid of\
you may ask me why did i mention ataxia here if it is a normal ability of a human being to discover.\
and, certainly, you will correct, expect for this one particular part\
ataxia of one's mind is when they do something unusual to escape the checkmate life has given them\
and i am more than assured that it happens at every stage of each person's life while they are working to develop / express their abilities and knowledge
