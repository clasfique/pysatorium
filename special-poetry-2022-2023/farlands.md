# Farlands

***

once upon a time pavlo comes with an idea to write something about his journeys\
but these journeys arent physical\
they are rather something that can be described as "imaginary journeys"

honestly, i just made up that term\

lets take a glance and immerse ourselves deeper

as far as i remember, they are like some nightmares, but it turns you wiser each time

they kind of scare me, because they are hard to go through\
but the point will always stay the same:\
we all are going to make it.
