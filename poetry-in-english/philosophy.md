The greatest blessing of me and the worst curse of me is the ability to calmly observe how the world I built falls apart

Unlike a plenty of people, physical experience for me is nothing, it only affects my physical condition\
However, the spiritual voyage goes far beyond that spectre\
I often sit on a bench and think to myself - why shall I think of myself when there are the ones in need?\
Why shall I be ungrateful for my life when there are those that die\
How painful it is for me to throw away even a little piece of bread - for it could have fed a one in famine\
My heart hurts for those that hate each other, especially the ones that are closely related\
And don't even get me started on shepherds that lose their sheep\
Truly I tell you, this is saddening to the roots of my heart\
Tears of mothers that won't see their sons\
And thus I thought to myself - how selfish am I if I think only about myself\
I do not need a company for when I walk alone, for my spirit shall find its place\
Truly I witness to my covenant with my body, I will do my maximum to truly love life, even with its difficulties\
I may not know why it was given to us or why is it here at all, however, I need to learn how to value\
The more I think to myself, how pleasing it is to listen to rainfall,birds and quiet flowers of steppes\
How pleasant is it to strip away anger for others, and to feel peace towards those who truly hate you\
And I shall say, I have not reached the point where I can love my enemies, for I have none, but shall the skies be witness to me giving love to those in need\
I am not afraid of sitting alone in the darkest day, I am afraid of not being honest to myself or in such lustful desire for money that I lose myself\
Harmony must prevail, in evil there is good, and in good there is evil
