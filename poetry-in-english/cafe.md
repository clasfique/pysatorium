# Cafe

***

I remember this cafe I always used to go to after my lectures in the school\
It's in the historical center of Lviv, but it is a very niche and small place,in fact, it's not even a cafe - it's a little sweet making manufactory\
I used to study there, go on dates - all of that within one year\
Sadness, happiness, the beginning of my poetry renewal project - all begun there\
And I think to myself that's the place that used to make me feel like home, I can't lie\
Except for the little inconvinience when some little gypsies tried to steal my coat\
Never the less, I've been reading books there, talking to people, drinking coffee\
And those times made my internal pain soften\
A cup of coffee and some night Lviv life distracted me from worries of life, somewhat\
And those moments make me reminiscent - of what I have lived so far\
I might be not even an adult, but I surely know something

...

Think of everything we have accomplished
