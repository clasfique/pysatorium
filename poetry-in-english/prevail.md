Quite some time ago, I was walking to my lecture of Ukrainian history\
The tram I used broke down, so I had to walk around the city for a little bit, since I had some time\
And it was quite cold and very weird to notice some little particles of life I haven't noticed\
I looked in the sky - it was gray, as a grain of basalt\
I questioned myself - how long is this going to be the case\
How long shall it take until I learn to prevail in the fight for myself\
For over 2 years I've been fighting for myself at that very point\
And for myself as in against myself\
Because I didn't have "enemies" when I used to be a bit isolated from my peers\
I imagined this ideal version of somebody who knows more than me, and I studied to prove that I know better\
So that was the way of mine to prevail\
It may be seen here that I used the way of Don Quixote,however he used to fight against the mills\
I have fought against myself, and I haven't won then, neither do I now, but I eventually manage to finally make the fights end up in a status quo, rather than in a defeat\
This is prevail for me, for I am getting closer of my personal victory
