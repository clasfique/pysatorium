# Prometheus

***

From dark forests shall I come

With the lights of the druids

With the flame of hope everlasting

Despite the currant I must witness

Despite the mourn of the Pantheon

How they will curse me with the throne

The light shall be brought upon the glum

To the depth of the dark my horse shall rise

To the moss of the moist I shall be best

How the slums of the forests I bestowed

The accepted me as a one

Non grata was I not

To those that fight - slums be like home

To those that hide - they save you in the dark

To those that invade, they are like trap

For locals know the spirit and a way, which outsiders shall not know

Prometheus , how far we have risen

The fire is spreading around the world

Bestowed we were by you

But the fire rises, further than it was predicted

The fire has undergone a lot, yet never was extinguished

Not for reasons that I know

But for blood and will of the glow

The glow of the fruit - as in people of the mud

They fight, they shine and they fall

...

Awaken, the people!

The crown has been given

In darkness shall we come

One shall bring all

Under flames of bonfire

The prophet of the fire will be chosen

And will be the one

To have the Earth unite

And for us, the kind

For the struggling

For the desiring of the soul

Home we will come
