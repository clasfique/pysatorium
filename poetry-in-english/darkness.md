I remembering returning form my studies with a map of Ukraine\
On that day I, as a very intelligent creature, decided to walk all around the city with the map forementioned\
And there is this forest that also has a railroad in it\
It hasn't been used since 2016 I believe\
However, I really love walking when it's really dark and snowy there\
it really makes me think of the lovely times of my 14-15th yearol of life\
And not that I am much older now, but life has spoken - bringing me places\
piano keys play in my mind\
and I remember talking to people while walking via the road\
it used to be my main road home\
I could have been beaten up there, because it's really dark, although not unsafe\
However, something continued luring me there\
Memories, childhood, sound of the wind\
I keep mentioning this theme, because I find it everwarming to my heart\
Nothing stays within my spirit as much as the forest - for my hideout it is, for the coldness I love
