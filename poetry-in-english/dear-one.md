# Dear one

***

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-12-30 170322.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Oh, thinking bewilding of you at night\
Reminiscing of the times of dance and flight\
No eyes hunt me down as much as these\
In which I saw peace and translucent love\
Quite laughs and walks in a forest of my childhood\
No matter where I go - I think of you\
And you know I do, I really do\
May my everlasting calmness empower me, for soul of mine is away\
Many ways were walked upon - none of them lead me right\
Flowers are in my eyes - reflection of you

Nights by the sea of howling winds\
Forming a tie between me and the sea\
Something as sincere as in books - experienced Once upon a life in light\
And shall I say, I mayn't express my light\
But for words were given to me - to show my love\
For you, the dear one\
For blessed I am for you
