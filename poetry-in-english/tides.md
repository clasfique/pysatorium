# Tides

***

When I was younger, I had an amazing opportunity to listen to the symphony of Adriatic sea\
I was sitting on a chair, look at the dark, somewhat promiscuous see, which showed me nothing other than darkness, thus becoming somewhat of a mirror to me\
Why a mirror one would ask, because you can see nothing in darkness, other than yourself and, for the forementioned reason ,you need to imagine the mirror yourself\
Somewhat an abstraction if you had to ask me\
But I tried to go deeper - beyond the mirror\
Somewhere as deep that I could easily get lost within the tides\
To put it frankly, it's like diving in cold water, however, in a more of a metaphorical meaning\
Nobody is going to warm you inside that little imagination\
It's just you and your reflection\
In its rawest form\
And all you can really do is just to talk to it\
Not even touch it, not even to show a tender intention



...

Then I returned to gazing at the sea - just to return myself from the realm\
I have learnt how to dive really deep within myself,which helped me to self-regulate various situations and odysseus for that matter, which is a bit fascinating, but proves my point even further\
water shall shape the rock, just you wait\
and really - the amount of experiments I have done on my metacognition is really disturbing yet astonishing\
for the sole purpose of explanation let's simplify it\
imagine a computer\
it consists of parts\
it's hard to replace these parts with knowledge, let alone without\
and now imagine me doing the same with my brain, but on metacognitive level - that's the most disturbing part of it\
I managed to create a sandbox within my own head - which works as a way to try out stuff without harming the mainframe - my brain itself, or, shall I say, the idea of a brain\
the other things I managed to replace - is quite literally the things that made my personality before, and now most of them are gone\
lobotomy might not be as useful in its physical format, but if I had to call something very similar to it in more metaphorical form - this would be it\
With all of this catharsis having said\
It would be proper to imagine human brain as a computer,parts of which can be replaced,repaired\
And you can even try to rebuild the "operating system" into something other\
Something scary or something very different\
All upon you, my sailor

in that place, we didn't realize that it's actually dangerous\
so my friend got down easily, meanwhile me,somehow, managed to fall on his lower part and get his clothes dirty\
I was really angry about that, but, nevertheless, we continued to walk

after one more hour of walking around unhinged ways, our journey was over

which was one of the very last of my journeys, before my life changed completely,which amazes me. Because I remember this voyage very vividly

The whole point of this story?\
Moments pass so quickly that sometimes it takes just a breath to realize - it's been 3 years
