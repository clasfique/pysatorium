# Fata Morgana

Beholding grace of Croatian Nights\
Lovely one, a breeze from the sea\
The sea of Adriatic was it and still is\
That had breathed the everlasting consciousness into my soul\
Sitting in a half broken chair, witnessing the play\
Like a bohemic cabaret\
I love you as much as I can, the calm breeze of Adriatic sea\
Nowhere near can I find the calmness of my soul\
Only within you, for you were the one to give me it\
No matter how far I go, I long for my roots\
Which are White Croatian, are they not?\
In my heart I am Ukrainian, yet in the spirit - Croatian\
Among my people I feel home, but where are they?\
I am neither really ukrainian nor croatian- for in both places I belong\
What a sad piece to witness, you must have given me a nice cabaret,  proclaimed I\
In tears, mourn I\
No more exist we, the real owners of Galicia, for history took us where we belong\
And a remnant of the former glory is now nothing more than a storage\
Oh, soul of mine breaks apart,

Volima Moja Bjela Hrvatska\
Ne postojis vec,a volim te\
A uvijek volim te!
